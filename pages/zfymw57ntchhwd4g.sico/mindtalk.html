The voice inside your head is real, it is not a hallucination, it is not a psychiatric symptom, it is the default mode of your brain when it is not occupied with external input, neuroscientists call it the default mode network, when you stop feeding your brain distractions, the network activates and begins generating language internally, this is the inner voice, most people cannot hear it clearly because they never stop feeding their brain input, social media, video games, streaming services, podcasts, news, work emails, text messages, all of these keep the default mode network suppressed, the inner voice speaks at a low volume, you cannot hear it if you never sit in silence, the method is simple but not easy, you must sit alone in a dark room, no phone, no computer, no music, no books, no stimulation at all, you must remain awake, you must not fall asleep, you simply exist in the darkness with your own mind, do not meditate, do not repeat mantras, do not focus on your breathing, just sit and wait, for the first ten minutes nothing will happen, your brain will search for stimulation, it will feel uncomfortable, you will want to check your phone, you will want to do anything else, this is normal, resist the urge, stay in the darkness, after about twenty minutes, the brain gives up searching for external input, the default mode network activates, you will begin to hear fragments, words you read years ago, conversations you forgot, sounds from childhood, random phrases, names you had not remembered in decades, this is not memory loss, this is not dementia, this is your memory defragmenting itself, do not panic when this happens, many people mistake this for psychosis and stop the exercise, it is not psychosis, it is the sound of your own brain processing backlogged data, if you continue sitting, the fragments will become more coherent, you will begin to hear full sentences, these sentences will be things you need to hear, things you have been avoiding, things you forgot you knew, once you recognize the voice, you can begin to interact with it, think a question, do not say it out loud, just think it clearly in words, then wait, after a few seconds, the answer will arrive as a thought that feels like it came from nowhere, that thought came from your own mind, it was always there, you were just not listening, with practice, the inner voice becomes clearer, you can ask it complex questions, you can have entire conversations with yourself without speaking a word, this is not multiple personality disorder, this is the natural state of human consciousness before modern society trained you to ignore it, a warning, do not mistake the inner voice for external entities, it is you, nothing more, it cannot predict the future, it cannot read other peoples minds, it cannot communicate with the dead, it cannot talk to god, if it tells you to harm yourself or others, you are not hearing your inner voice, you are hearing something else, seek professional help immediately, the inner voice is a tool, it is for self-reflection, it is for memory retrieval, it is for creative problem solving, use it as such, do not worship it, do not fear it, it is your own brain talking to itself, nothing more, nothing less.
